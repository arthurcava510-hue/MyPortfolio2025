Arthur Rey Cava

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Hello I'm Arthur Rey Cava. Born in the province of Zambales, my passion for technology began at an early age when I spent much of my time in my brother’s computer shop. He also dreamed of becoming an IT professional, and being surrounded by computers, gadgets, and curious minds inspired me to follow the same path. From that point on, I knew I wanted to work with technology — to understand how it functions and how it can make life better.

During high school, I sharpened my skills in programming, joining competitions, and exploring various technologies that motivated me to deepen my knowledge and improve my skill.

My dream is to become a leading frontend developer who creates meaningful user experiences and innovative interfaces that inspire users and solve real problems.

Outside of coding, I love to travel, capture moments through photography, and learn about new cultures. Balance in life helps me stay creative and productive.

I believe that technology must be accessible to all, and great design should simplify complexity. My goal is to create with empathy and precision, putting users at the heart of development.
